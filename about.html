Our Story

Founded in 2022, Elly Smart Gadgets emerged from a simple idea: technology should be intuitive, elegant, and empowering. What started as a small startup in Kampala has grown into a trusted brand serving thousands of customers across Uganda.

We believe that smart living should be accessible to everyone — not just the few. That's why we combine affordability with premium quality, ensuring our gadgets genuinely improve daily life.
